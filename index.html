How to Use · Nasıl Kullanılır

Up and running in 30 seconds.

30 saniyede başla.

1
🌍
Download & Choose Your Language
İndir ve dilini seç

On first launch, PekiSor detects your device language automatically. Confirm it or pick from Turkish, English, German, French, or Spanish — and you're in.

2
🎯
Pick a Category
Bir kategori seç

Browse 13 themed categories on the home screen — from Philosophical and Root Questions to Kids and Light & Silly. Tap any card to start playing immediately.

3
🃏
Choose Your Mode
Modunu seç

Pool — draws from the full question bank for that category. Favorites — plays back only the questions you've saved. Switch anytime at the top of the home screen.

4
💬
Read, Reflect & Discuss
Oku, düşün, konuş

A question appears on screen. Read it aloud, take turns answering, or just let it spark a conversation. Tap Next Question whenever you're ready to move on.

5
❤️
Save Your Favorites
Favorilerine ekle

Tap the ❤️ heart button on any question to save it. All your favorites are stored privately on your device — no account needed, never shared.

6
↗️
Share a Beautiful Card
Güzel bir kart paylaş

Tap ↗ Share to generate a beautifully designed question card. Send it to a friend, post it to your story, or drop it into a group chat to start the conversation before you even meet.

💡

Pro tip: Switch your language from the Settings tab at any time — all questions, categories, and the interface update instantly. Great for multilingual families and international friend groups.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

PekiSor ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

PekiSor collects minimal data:

  • Language preference — stored locally on your device only
  • Theme preference (light/dark) — stored locally on your device only
  • Favorite questions — stored locally on your device only

We do not collect personal information, location data, or usage analytics. All data remains on your device.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. We have no advertising partners and display no advertisements.

4. Children's Privacy

PekiSor includes content suitable for children (Kids category). We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information.

5. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information is protected by your device's own security measures.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: [email protected]

8. Gizlilik Politikası (Türkçe Özet)

PekiSor uygulaması hiçbir kişisel veri toplamaz. Dil tercihiniz, tema ayarınız ve favori sorularınız yalnızca cihazınızda yerel olarak saklanır. Reklam gösterilmez, veri paylaşılmaz.
